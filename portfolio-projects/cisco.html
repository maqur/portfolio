Cisco University Challenge

Overview

Cisco university challenge was a 24 hour competition in which each team from universities across the UK had to develop a working prototype of a product based on one to 4 challenges derived from real world business problems related to Internet of Everything (IoE).

Design and Implementation

My group decided to build a travel recommendations app that would be integrated with the user's social media profiles and based on their preferences and likings recommend suitable travel destinations. It would also be synced with the user's calendar, check for upcomming holidays and search for best deals for that time. We integrated our web app with facebook using it's API to get user's preferences and likes information. Due to time constraints some technologies like machine learning for more relavent suggestions could not be implemented.

Outcome

We managed to develop enough for a proof of concept in the given 24 hours. We were runners up among 24 team in the competition.

This competition was my first hackathon. It was a great experiance meeting and socializing with people from many different backgrounds. It was both challenging and fun working on the product under pressure. I got familiar with serveral web technologies like Nodejs, Facebook API, etc which I had not used before.
